About Hurricane Melissa

Hurricane Melissa made landfall in Jamaica on October 28, 2025, as a catastrophic Category 5 storm, the most powerful in the island's history.

Devon Loves Jamaica is a grassroots digital campaign raising awareness and funds to support those in need.

Founded 2025 by the family descendants of Devon Jamaican-English Footballer Jack Leslie

Immediate Needs

  • Food
  • Clean water
  • Shelter
  • Medical aid
  • Infrastructure repair
  • Donations
  • Volunteers

Affected Areas

  • Westmoreland (including New Hope)
  • St. Elizabeth (including the Black River)
  • St. James (including Montego Bay)
  • Hanover
  • Manchester
  • Rural agricultural areas
  • infrastructure across the island
